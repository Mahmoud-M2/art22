WHO ARE WE.

Hello,

I am Bashayr , an artist specializing in classical painting on fabrics and canvases.

I draw my inspiration from nature and the beauty of the classical era, which is reflected in every piece of art I create. My passion for fabrics goes beyond traditional boundaries of art, as I strive to blend classical elegance with unique artistic touches. I take great care in selecting and detailing each material I work with, ensuring that every piece is a product of unique creativity and high taste. I value distinction and have a deep appreciation for vintage items that add a special charm and authenticity to my work. I invite you to explore my artistic world that combines classicism with modernity, and creativity in fabrics and clothing. You can view my work and contact me through the following links.

    Classical art is a manifestation of the creative spirit that has spanned the ages, reflecting balance, beauty, and precision in its details. Classical art is characterized by its focus on ideal forms, harmony, and correct proportions, expressing an artistic vision that strives for perfection and absolute beauty.

         Classical art mirrors ancient civilizations such as Greek and Roman, where there was a profound interest in depicting the human body with precise and idealized accuracy. Classical artworks, whether paintings or sculptures, are marked by elegance and harmony, celebrating natural beauty and human strength.

         Through classical art, we can explore ancient worlds and understand the philosophies and ideas that shaped those eras. Studying this art deepens our understanding of history and culture, opening doors to inspiration and creativity

         Classical art is not just about beautiful works; it is a journey into the past and a reminder of the values of beauty and perfection we have strived to achieve

         It remains a source of inspiration for artists and art enthusiasts around the world, maintaining its place as a symbol of timeless beauty and high creativity.
